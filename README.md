## Business Use Case: why we predict Grand Prix outcomes
Predicting F1 Qualifying and Race outcomes can give valuable insights for Formula 1 stakeholders, teams, athletes and fans alike. 

### Context
The Formula 1 season has two parallel championships: the Driver championship and the Constructor championship. Both have a great impact for the involved parties. Predicting Grand Prix can help them prepare and have an idea of their own overall performance, and on their competitors' performance. It can also give fans insights and tips about what's to come on the weekend.
